The Legend Hanoi

Days 2–4 • Mon 5 – Wed 7 Jan • Hanoi

Daily Activities

  • Day 2 – Mon 5 Jan: A very early 5:30am check-in at the Legend Hotel! Toured with Milena and local guide Mr Lap, visiting the French Quarter, Tran Quoc Pagoda, Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, and the One Pillar Pagoda. Finished with dinner at Little Hanoi and a walk around the local area.
  • Day 3 – Tue 6 Jan: 8:15am start. Explored Train Street and the Temple of Literature, followed by the National Fine Arts Museum. Lunch was at Know One, Teach One. Evening highlights included the Water Puppet Theatre and the historic Old Quarter, with dinner at another Little Hanoi.
  • Day 4 – Wed 7 Jan: 8:30am start. Visited the Pearl factory and King Kong Skull Island. Enjoyed a Halong Bay tour with a lunch cruise and a visit to the Dong Thien Chung Caves. Dinner back at The Legend Hotel.
