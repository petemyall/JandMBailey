Indochine Palace, Huế

Days 5–6 • Thu 8 – Fri 9 Jan • Huế

Daily Activities

  • Day 5 – Thu 8 Jan: 8:45am start (bags out early). Mr. Lap led a tour to Hoa Lo Prison (Hanoi Hilton). Jan enjoyed a massage while Merlin revisited Train Street. Lunch at Spices Villa before flying to Hue. Met new guide Quang and driver Phu; checked into Indochine Palace Hotel and walked to Madam Thu restaurant.
  • Day 6 – Fri 9 Jan: 8:30am start with Quang. Boat trip (45 mins) along the Perfume River (Huong River) to Thien Mu Pagoda. Explored the Imperial City (Hue) followed by lunch at Little Italy. Afternoon visiting the Tomb of Tu Duc and Mausoleum of Emperor Khai Dinh. Early night—no dinner! 😂
